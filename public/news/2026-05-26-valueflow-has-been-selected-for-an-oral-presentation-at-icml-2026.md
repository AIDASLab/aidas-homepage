We are excited to share that our paper, **VALUEFLOW: Toward Pluralistic and Steerable Value-based Alignment in Large Language Models**, has been selected for an **oral presentation** at **The International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML 2026)**.

This recognition highlights the significance of our work on pluralistic value-based alignment in large language models. **VALUEFLOW** introduces a unified framework spanning value extraction, evaluation, and steering with calibrated intensity control, enabling more fine-grained and controllable value alignment.
