We are excited to share that our paper, **Medic-AD: Towards Medical Vision-Language Model's Clinical Intelligence**, has been selected for an **oral presentation** at **CVPR 2026**.

**Medic-AD** introduces a medical vision-language framework for clinically grounded anomaly detection, symptom tracking, and visual explainability in real-world medical imaging settings.

Paper:

- [**Medic-AD: Towards Medical Vision-Language Model's Clinical Intelligence** — Woohyeon Park*, Jaeik Kim, Sunghwan Steve Cho, Pa Hong, Woo Kyoung Jeong, Yoojin Nam, Nam-Joon Kim, Ginny Wong, Ka Chun Cheung, Jaeyoung Do^](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.27176)

Congratulations to all the authors!
