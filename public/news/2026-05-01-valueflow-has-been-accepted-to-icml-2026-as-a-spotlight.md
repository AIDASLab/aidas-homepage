We are excited to share that our paper, **VALUEFLOW: Toward Pluralistic and Steerable Value-based Alignment in Large Language Models**, has been accepted to **The International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML 2026)** as a **Spotlight**.

**VALUEFLOW** introduces a unified framework for pluralistic value-based alignment, covering value extraction, evaluation, and steering with calibrated intensity control. It combines **HiVES**, a hierarchical value embedding space, the **Value Intensity DataBase (VIDB)**, and an anchor-based evaluator to study and control how LLMs express values across multiple value theories.
